Ba  ·  Arabic Letter 2nd

The letter Ba — The container, the house, the vessel that holds — the first letter of Bismillah

Ba is the first letter spoken in the Quran — Bismillah begins with Ba.

Ilm ul Huroof  ·  The Letter Ba

Ba is the letter of the bayt — the house, the vessel, the receptive form. Classical scholars place Ba as the second letter because all manifestation requires a container, and Ba supplies that ontological vessel. A name carrying Ba carries a posture of hospitality and structural carrying-capacity.

The Letter Itself

What Ba encodes

Ba is the first letter spoken in the Quran — Bismillah begins with Ba. Its form is a horizontal vessel with a single point beneath it, like a boat or a cup. It is the container letter: the principle of enclosure, holding, and the relationship between what contains and what is contained.

Ilm ul Huroof

Ibn Arabi and the Science of Letters

Ibn Arabi wrote that Ba is the letter through which divine mercy first entered creation — Bismillah ir-Rahman ir-Rahim begins with Ba, and before the Ba is said to be a space of infinite meaning. The Sufi tradition holds that the entire Quran is in Al-Fatiha, Al-Fatiha is in Bismillah, and Bismillah is in the Ba — making Ba the seed of the entire revelation. It connects to Al-Batin — the Hidden, the Interior.

"Ba as a first letter governs the outward self — the primary mode through which the person meets the world and is met by it. Everything else in the name operates within the field this letter establishes."

Ilm ul Huroof · The Science of Letters
In a Name

Ba as the first letter

A person whose name begins with Ba is oriented toward holding, protecting, and nurturing what is entrusted to them. They are the ones others bring things to — children, secrets, responsibilities. They carry well. The vessel quality means they receive and hold deeply, but they must be careful not to carry what is not theirs to carry. Ba names are often found among those who become pillars of their families and communities.

Quranic Resonance

Ba in the Quran

Ba opens the entire Quran in Bismillah. It also appears in بسم which begins every surah. The letter of containers opens the greatest container of divine knowledge.
