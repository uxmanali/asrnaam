Privacy Policy

Asrnaam is a free, ad-free reading library. We try to collect as little personal information as possible.

What we collect

When you visit Asrnaam, our hosting provider receives basic technical data (IP address, browser type, page requested). We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand which pages people find useful. GA4 stores anonymised usage data — page views, country, device type — but no personally identifying information.

Name readings

If you type a name into the reading tool, the input is processed in your browser and is not sent to or stored on our servers.

Cookies

We use cookies set by Google Analytics for aggregate usage measurement. You can block these in your browser settings without breaking any feature of the site.

Email contact

If you email hello@asrnaam.com, we use that email only to respond to your message. We don't add you to any mailing list and don't share your address with anyone.

Third parties

We embed Google Fonts and Google Analytics. Their respective privacy policies govern those services. We don't run advertising and don't sell user data.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any data tied to your email by writing to hello@asrnaam.com.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the site evolves. Material changes will be noted on this page.
