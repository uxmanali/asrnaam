## Quality bar (per spec)
- Arabic: MSA / Classical, established Islamic terminology
- Urdu: formal, Nastaliq-friendly (not Hindi-leaning), standard Islamic register
- Famous bearers: properly translated names (e.g. خالد بن الوليد), Western numerals for AR dates, Urdu numerals for UR
- Pronunciation IPA: keep Latin as-is
- IUH letter readings: standard Arabic letter terminology (العين, الراء, etc.)
- NEVER fabricate. Adapt when sources thin.
