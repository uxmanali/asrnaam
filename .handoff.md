- Arabic: MSA / Classical; established Islamic terminology
- Urdu: formal, Nastaliq-friendly, Islamic register
- Bearers: properly translated names with Western numeral dates (e.g. 1234–1301م / ء)
- IPA: kept Latin in English_pronunciation field (Aabid)
- Famous bearer paragraphs translate properly; Quranic citations preserve surah/ayat numbers
- NEVER fabricate; adapt rather than invent where translation doesn't map cleanly
