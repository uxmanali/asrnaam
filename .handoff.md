## Quality bar (unchanged)
- Arabic: MSA / Classical; established Islamic terminology
- Urdu: formal, Nastaliq-friendly, Islamic register (no Hindi-leaning vocab)
- Bearers: properly translated names, AR uses Eastern-Arabic numerals from this batch onward where culturally apt; UR uses Urdu numerals
- IPA: keep Latin
- Never fabricate. Adapt with same intent when sources thin.

## Hreflang & sitemap invariants
- EN canonicals: hreflang chain (en/ar/ur/x-default) only when AR+UR pages exist. c1_runner.update_hreflang() is idempotent — won't double-insert.
- Untranslated EN canonicals: kept the C1-final stance (no ar/ur hreflang).
- Sitemap: append before `</urlset>`. Dedupe is built in.
