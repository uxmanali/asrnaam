ابن رشد کون تھے؟

علمی ورثہ

ابن رشد نے کیا چھوڑا

Ibn Rushd's defence of philosophy was rejected by parts of the religious establishment of his own time, he was briefly exiled, but his Latin commentaries became indispensable to medieval Christian scholasticism. Thomas Aquinas debated him directly. The Renaissance revival of Aristotle would not have happened without him. Scholarly assessment of his place within Islamic intellectual tradition remains debated to this day.
