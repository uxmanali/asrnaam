Dictionary Meaning

What Naira means

Radiant, brilliant, shining — from root ن-ي-ر (nayara — to shine brilliantly, to be radiant). نيرة (Naira — the radiant one, the brilliantly shining woman). The name carries the quality of brilliant, self-generated luminosity — not the reflected light of the moon but the active shining of the star.

Naira is the #30 most searched Muslim baby name on QuranicNames.com in 2026 (19%), trending across the Arab world, South Asia, and global Muslim communities. It has a beautiful sound and a meaning that combines Islamic light imagery with active brilliance.

PronunciationNAI-ra  ·  nai·ra  ·  2 syllables

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Naira reveals

Nun of depth opens, Alif of origin, Ya of reaching, Ra of mercy. A light reaching out as mercy.

People often notice a quiet inward life, thinking long before speaking in those who carry this name.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run watchful, inward, present in a quiet way. The Ya that runs through the name points to a reaching, aspirational interior — someone who draws people upward toward what they're moving toward. The closing Ta suggests a person who completes things cleanly, with few loose ends.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Naira

What does the name Naira mean?
Naira means: Radiant, brilliant — the woman who shines with pure luminous light. From root ن-ي-ر (brilliantly radiant, shining). The woman who shines with pure luminous light. #30 most searched Muslim girl name 2026 (19%). Beautiful sound with deep Islamic light imagery.
Is Naira mentioned in the Quran?
Root ن-ي-ر (to shine brilliantly) is related to noor (light) and siraj (lamp). 'He made the sun a radiance (dhiya)' (10:5). 'And the moon as a light (nuran)' (10:5). The Quran's light imagery encompasses every form of brilliance. Naira carries this quality of active shining.
What does Naira mean in Urdu?
Naira in Urdu is نیرہ (Naira) meaning چمکدار، روشن — radiant, brilliant, shining with light.
Is Naira a good Islamic name?
Yes, Naira is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Radiant, brilliant — the woman who shines with pure luminous light. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Naira a girl or boy name?
Naira is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Radiant, brilliant — the woman who shines with pure luminous light.
What is the spiritual meaning of Naira in Ilm ul Huroof?
Nun (ن) holds in primordial depth, Ya (ي) reaches, Ra (ر) moves with mercy, Ha (ه) breathes presence. Radiant: deep-sourced, reaching, merciful, divinely present — active brilliance.
