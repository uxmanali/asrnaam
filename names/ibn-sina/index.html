Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Ibn Sina joins fire to earth, the will that decides with the patience that lasts. This is a name that asks its bearer to burn steadily rather than in bursts, and to plant what the fire has cleared. Running through the reading is Al-Ahad, the One, carried as a singular, undivided presence. A person named Ibn Sina plants what their fire has cleared.

Section 5 · Bearers

Who Has Carried This Name

  • Abū ʿAlī al-Ḥusayn ibn ʿAbd Allāh ibn al-Ḥasan ibn ʿAlī ibn Sīnā (980 – 1037 CE · Bukhara / Hamadan / Isfahan). Ibn Sīnā's al-Qānūn fī al-Ṭibb (The Canon of Medicine) systematised Greek, Indian, and Arab medical knowledge into a single five-book encyclopedia that became the standard medical text in Europe and the Muslim world for six centuries. His Kitāb al-Shifāʾ is a complete philosophical encyclopedia covering logic, natural science, mathematics, and metaphysics in the Aristotelian tradition adapted to Islamic thought. He served as physician and vizier to several rulers and wrote more than a hundred works.

Frequently Asked Questions

About Ibn Sina

Who was Ibn Sina?
Ibn Sina (980 – 1037 CE) was al-Shaykh al-Raʾīs. The Chief Master (Avicenna). Abū ʿAlī al-Ḥusayn ibn ʿAbd Allāh ibn al-Ḥasan ibn ʿAlī ibn Sīnā. Active in Bukhara / Hamadan / Isfahan, field: Medicine, philosophy, natural science.
What does the name Ibn Sina mean?
Ibn Sina means: 'Son of Sīnā', a family name of Persian origin.
Is Ibn Sina an Islamic name?
Yes. Ibn Sina is a recognised Arabic name of significant historical weight in the Muslim world, borne by al-Shaykh al-Raʾīs. The Chief Master (Avicenna).
What does Ibn Sina mean in Urdu?
Ibn Sina in Urdu is written ابن سینا. 'سینا کا بیٹا', فارسی الاصل خاندانی نام۔
What region was Ibn Sina associated with?
Bukhara / Hamadan / Isfahan.
