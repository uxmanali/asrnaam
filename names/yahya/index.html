Dictionary Meaning

What Yahya means

John the Baptist — named directly by Allah in the Quran. Yahya (AS) is the son of Zakariya (AS), born to elderly parents as a divine miracle. Allah named him directly: 'O Zakariya, We give you glad tidings of a boy whose name is Yahya — We have never given that name to anyone before' (19:7). He was given wisdom in childhood: 'O Yahya, take the Scripture with determination. And We gave him judgement while yet a child' (19:12). He is described in the Quran as 'sayyidan' (a leader), 'hasura' (chaste), and 'nabiyyan' (a prophet).

The Portrait

What Yahya asks of its bearer

People named Yahya carry a quality of living, breathing, double-reaching vitality — like their namesake who gave life to others before his own was taken.

A Notable Bearer

Yahya (AS) — 'We have never given that name to anyone before' (19:7). The only prophet in the Quran whose name was chosen directly by Allah. Martyred for his faith.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Yahya

What does the name Yahya mean?
Yahya means: John the Baptist — named directly by Allah, given wisdom in childhood. Named directly by Allah (19:7): 'We have never given that name to anyone before.' Given wisdom in childhood (19:12). Sayyid (leader), hasur (chaste), nabi (prophet). Son of Zakariya (AS).
Is Yahya mentioned in the Quran?
Yes — 'O Zakariya, We give you glad tidings of a boy whose name is Yahya — We have never given that name to anyone before' (19:7). Directly Quranic. Allah chose the name personally.
What does Yahya mean in Urdu?
Yahya in Urdu is یحییٰ (Yahya) meaning یحیی نبی — زکریا کے بیٹے.
Is Yahya a good Islamic name?
Yes, Yahya is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: John the Baptist — named directly by Allah, given wisdom in childhood. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Yahya a boy or girl name?
Yahya is traditionally a Muslim boy name meaning John the Baptist — named directly by Allah, given wisdom in childhood.
What is the spiritual meaning of Yahya in Ilm ul Huroof?
Double Ya reaches twice, Ha breathes life, Ya arrives. Yahya: reaching and reaching, breathing life, completely arrived.
