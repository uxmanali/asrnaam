Dictionary Meaning

What Kerim means

کریم Kerim کا مطلب: سخی، فیاض۔ اللہ تعالٰی کا صفاتی نام "الکریم"۔

Kerim is the Turkish spelling of Kareem. Al-Kareem is one of the 99 names of Allah: 'The Most Generous, The Noble.' The Quran itself is called al-Quran al-Kareem, the Noble Quran. When used as a name of Allah's attribute the convention is to prefix 'Abd' (Abdul Kareem).

Root of Kerim

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Kerim reveals

کَرِيم
Kaf ک sufficiency, "kun" – Be.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Meem م the maternal, water.

Kaf brings divine sufficiency, Ra moves in mercy, Ya reaches, Meem is maternal. Generosity as posture.
