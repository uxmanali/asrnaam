About the name

Umm Salamah is an Arabic name, drawn from the classical Arabic lexicographic tradition (Lisan al-Arab, al-Sihah, Taj al-Arus). The name carries the meaning: The mother of Salamah. One of the wives of the Prophet.

Every letter of Umm Salamah in the Arabic script has been read in the classical tradition of Ilm ul Huroof as an ontological principle: the letters do not merely spell the sound of the name, they carry the qualities the tradition ascribes to it. See the letter reading above.

In Islam, the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "You will be called on the Day of Resurrection by your names and the names of your fathers, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud). A well-chosen name is a lifelong invocation of the meaning it carries.
