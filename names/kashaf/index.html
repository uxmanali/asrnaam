Dictionary Meaning

What Kashaf means

کشف Kashaf کا مطلب: انکشاف، حقیقت کھولنے والی، پردہ اٹھانے والی۔

Kashaf names the one who brings hidden truths to light. In classical Sufi vocabulary, kashf is the technical term for the unveiling of spiritual reality granted by Allah to the sincere heart. Al-Kashshaf is a famous 12th-century exegetical work (Al-Zamakhshari's tafsir). As a girls' name Kashaf carries this dimension: a life dedicated to seeing and revealing what is real.
