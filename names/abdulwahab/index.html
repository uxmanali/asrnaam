Section 1 · Arabic Root

Where the Name Comes From

و-ه-ب

و-ه-ب. From w-h-b, to give freely, to bestow, wahhāb is the constant bestower, al-Wahhāb a name of God. Joined with ʿabd (servant) from ʿ-b-d.

Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Abdulwahab's letters draw from many elements at once, a full palette, not a single note. This is a name that asks its bearer to hold contradictions gracefully: warmth and steadiness, movement and depth. Running through the reading is Al-Alim, the All-Knowing, carried by a person as a knowing that predates study. A person named Abdulwahab lives with more inside them than any one word can hold.

Section 4 · What Families Say

What People Usually Mean by This Name

Abdulwahab (عبد الوهاب) is composed of two Arabic words: ʿabd, servant, wholly at the disposal of another, and the divine Name الوهاب, the Bestower, the One who gives without expectation of return. The full name therefore declares its bearer the servant of the Bestower, the One who gives without expectation of return.

"Our Lord, do not let our hearts deviate after You have guided us, and grant us from Yourself mercy. Indeed, You are the Bestower" (3:8). The Prophet ﷺ said, in a hadith reported by Abu Dawud and Ibn Mājah: "The most beloved of names to Allah are ʿAbdullāh and ʿAbd ar-Raḥmān", and the classical scholars extended that love to every name of the form ʿAbd-X where X is one of the asmāʾ al-ḥusnā. The romanizations vary: Abdulwahab is the Maghrebi (North African) form, while Abdul Wahhab predominates in the Arab East and South Asia. The same root letters and the same theological meaning sit beneath every spelling.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Abdulwahab

What does the name Abdulwahab mean?
Abdulwahab means: Servant of Al-Wahhab, the Bestower. Servant of Al-Wahhab, the abd-name structure. 'The best names are Abdullah and Abdurrahman' (Abu Dawud). Al-Wahhab (the Bestower) in Quran: "Our Lord, let not our hearts deviate... and grant us from Yourself mercy. Indeed You are the Bestower (Al-Wahhab)" (3:8).
Is Abdulwahab mentioned in the Quran?
Al-Wahhab (the Bestower) in Quran: "Our Lord, let not our hearts deviate... and grant us from Yourself mercy. Indeed You are the Bestower (Al-Wahhab)" (3:8). Every abd-name dedicates the bearer to the divine quality in his name.
What does Abdulwahab mean in Urdu?
Abdulwahab in Urdu is عبدالوهاب (Abdulwahab) meaning اللہ کا بندہ. Servant of Al-Wahhab.
Is Abdulwahab a good Islamic name?
Yes, Abdulwahab is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Servant of Al-Wahhab, the Bestower. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Abdulwahab a boy or girl name?
Abdulwahab is traditionally a Muslim boy name meaning Servant of Al-Wahhab, the Bestower.
What is the spiritual meaning of Abdulwahab in Ilm ul Huroof?
Ain (perceiving spring). Ba (vessel). Dal (returning door): the servant perceives, holds, and returns. Then Al-Wahhab (Al-Wahhab): the Bestower.
