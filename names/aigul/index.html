Dictionary Meaning

What Aigul means

آئیگل Aigul کا مطلب: چاند کا پھول۔ ترکی-تاتاری مسلم اسم۔

Aigul, from Turkic ay (moon) + Persian-Turkic gül (flower, rose), names the girl as a "flower of the moon." It is among the most-given female names in Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Bashkortostan, Tatarstan, and other Turkic-Muslim republics. In Cyrillic: Айгуль.
