Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Muhammed

ح-م-د — From ḥ-m-d, to praise. Muḥammad is the most-praised, the passive intensive of ḥamida. Muhammed is the Turkish and modern Arab spelling.

About the name Muhammed

What does the name Muhammed mean?
Muhammed means: The Most Praised — common South Asian and Turkish variant. It is a spelling variant of Muhammad — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name محمد.
Is Muhammed the same as Muhammad?
Yes — Muhammed and Muhammad are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Muhammed a good Islamic name?
Yes, Muhammed is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Muhammad (محمد) — The Most Praised — common South Asian and Turkish variant.
Which spelling is correct — Muhammed or Muhammad?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name محمد is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
