Dictionary Meaning

What Siddiq means

The truthful, the one who affirms truth — from root s-d-q (sadaqa — to be truthful). Al-Siddiq is the title given to Abu Bakr after he immediately believed the Prophet account of the Night Journey when others doubted. 'Do not grieve — indeed Allah is with us' (9:40).

Siddiq names the man of perfect truthfulness — whose word is his bond, who confirms what is right immediately. The Quran: 'And those who believe in Allah and His messengers — those are the Siddiqun (the truthful ones)' (57:19). Top Pakistani and Arab boys name.
