Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Leyla

What does the name Leyla mean?
Leyla means: Night — the Turkish spelling of the beloved name. It is a spelling variant of Layla — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name ليلى.
Is Leyla the same as Layla?
Yes — Leyla and Layla are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Leyla a good Islamic name?
Yes, Leyla is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Layla (ليلى) — Night — the Turkish spelling of the beloved name.
Which spelling is correct — Leyla or Layla?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name ليلى is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
