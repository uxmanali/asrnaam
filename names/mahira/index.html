Dictionary Meaning

What Mahira means

Skilled, expert, gifted, talented — feminine of ماهر (mahir — skilled, expert, the one proficient in their craft). From root م-ه-ر (mahara — to be skilled, to be proficient). The Quran uses this root: 'Al-saffatu bil-Qurani al-karim — The skillful (mahir) in reciting the Quran will be with the noble scribes' (Hadith, Muslim).

Mahira is one of the top 15 most searched Muslim girl names in Pakistan, particularly prominent through Mahira Khan — one of the most celebrated Pakistani actresses globally. The name carries the quality of the woman of genuine skill and capability — whose excellence is the result of both natural talent and devoted effort.

The Letters

Reading Mahira through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

مMeemFirst · Outward SelfاAlifInteriorهHaInteriorرRaInteriorهHaLast · Resolution

Mahira opens with Meem (م) — love and depth. Then Alif (ا) — origin. Then Ha (ه) — divine presence. Then Ra (ر) — perpetual mercy. Then Ha (ه) — divine presence again. Love and depth — origin — divine presence — perpetual mercy — divine presence. The skilled woman: held in love, rooted in origin, breathing divine presence, moving with perpetual mercy, breathing divine presence again. She breathes the divine twice — her skill is divinely animated.

"A name is not a label assigned after the fact. It is a structure of principles that begins its work the moment it is given."

The Portrait

What Mahira asks of its bearer

People named Mahira tend to be genuinely excellent at what they do — their skill is evident and authentic. The Meem holds in love. The Alif grounds in origin. The Ha breathes divine presence: her skill is divinely given. The Ra moves with perpetual mercy. The final Ha breathes divine presence again: her excellence is doubly animated.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Mahira

What does the name Mahira mean?
Mahira means: Skilled, expert, gifted — the woman of extraordinary capability and talent. Feminine of Mahir — from root م-ه-ر (skilled, expert). The gifted, skilled woman of genuine capability. One of the top 15 most searched Muslim girl names in Pakistan.
Is Mahira mentioned in the Quran?
The root of Mahira (م-ه-ر) appears in the hadith: 'The one skillful (mahir) in reciting the Quran will be with the noble, righteous scribes' (Muslim). The concept of God-given skill appears throughout the Quran in descriptions of prophets: 'We taught him the making of armour for you' (21:80).
What does Mahira mean in Urdu?
Mahira in Urdu is ماہرہ (Mahira) meaning ماہر، قابل، ہنرمند — skilled, expert, gifted, talented.
Is Mahira a good Islamic name?
Yes, Mahira is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Skilled, expert, gifted — the woman of extraordinary capability and talent. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Mahira a girl or boy name?
Mahira is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Skilled, expert, gifted — the woman of extraordinary capability and talent.
What is the spiritual meaning of Mahira in Ilm ul Huroof?
Meem (م) holds in love, Alif (ا) grounds in origin, double Ha (ه) breathes divine presence twice, Ra (ر) moves with perpetual mercy. The skilled woman: loving, originary, doubly divine, merciful.
