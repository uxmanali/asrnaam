Section 1 · Arabic Root

Where the Name Comes From

ر-ش-د

ر-ش-د, feminine of Rashīd, from r-sh-d, to be rightly-guided. Rashīda is the rightly-guided woman.

Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Rashida weaves earth and water, the elements of steadiness and mercy, patience and feeling. The person is asked to be both root and river, a slow, sheltering presence. Running through the reading is Al-Rahman, the Merciful, meaning the bearer is trusted with mercy that keeps moving even when tested, joined by Al-Tawwab, the Relenting, carried as the readiness to return and be returned to. A person named Rashida holds their people through seasons.

Section 4 · What Families Say

What People Usually Mean by This Name

Wise, rightly guided, feminine form of Rashid. Widely used in Swahili East African Muslim communities and across the African continent. Al-Rashid (the Guide to Right Path) is a divine name. Rashida carries the quality of the woman who is herself rightly guided and guides others.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Rashida

What does the name Rashida mean?
Rashida means: Wise, rightly guided. Swahili East African Muslim feminine name. Feminine of Rashid. Al-Rashid, divine name. East African Swahili Muslim communities. The rightly-guided woman who guides others.
Is Rashida mentioned in the Quran?
Al-Rashid, the Guide to the Right Path, is a divine name. 'He who follows the Messenger has been rightly guided' (3:132). Rashida aspires to this quality.
What does Rashida mean in Urdu?
Rashida in Urdu is رشیدہ (Rashida) meaning صحیح راستے پر, rightly guided, wise.
Is Rashida a good Islamic name?
Yes, Rashida is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Wise, rightly guided. Swahili East African Muslim feminine name. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Rashida a girl or boy name?
Rashida is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Wise, rightly guided. Swahili East African Muslim feminine name.
What is the spiritual meaning of Rashida in Ilm ul Huroof?
Ra moves with mercy, Sheen radiates, Ya reaches, Dal returns, Ta manifests. Rightly guided: merciful, radiant, reaching, returning, manifest.
