Dictionary Meaning

What Mariyah means

Radiant white — Mariyah al-Qibtiyya (RA), the Coptic Egyptian woman who was sent as a gift to the Prophet ﷺ by the Muqawqis (the ruler of Egypt) in 628 CE. She accepted Islam. She gave birth to the Prophet's son Ibrahim, who died as an infant. The Prophet was deeply grieved at Ibrahim's death. On the day Ibrahim died, there was a solar eclipse, and some said it was because of his death — the Prophet said: 'The sun and moon do not eclipse for the death or life of anyone. When you see them, pray.'.

Reading Mariyah through Ilm ul Huroof
