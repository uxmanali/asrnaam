Dictionary Meaning

What Shamsa means

Sun — from Arabic shams (sun) + ta marbuta. Shamsa is the feminine noun form of Shams. The sun in the Quran is one of the great divine signs: 'And the sun — We have made it a blazing lamp (siraj wahhaj)' (78:13). Surah Ash-Shams (Chapter 91) is named for the sun. Shamsa carries the quality of the woman who is herself a warm, blazing, life-giving solar presence.

Reading Shamsa through Ilm ul Huroof

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Shamsa

What does the name Shamsa mean?
Shamsa means: Sun — the solar feminine name of brilliant warm radiance. Feminine of Shams — the sun. Surah Ash-Shams (Chapter 91). 'We have made it a blazing lamp (siraj wahhaj)' (78:13). The solar woman.
Is Shamsa mentioned in the Quran?
Yes — Surah Ash-Shams (Chapter 91): 'By the sun and its brightness' (91:1). 'And the sun runs to its fixed resting place' (36:38). Shamsa is directly Quranic.
What does Shamsa mean in Urdu?
Shamsa in Urdu is شمسہ (Shamsa) meaning سورج، روشنی — the sun, radiant warmth.
Is Shamsa a good Islamic name?
Yes, Shamsa is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Sun — the solar feminine name of brilliant warm radiance. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Shamsa a girl or boy name?
Shamsa is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Sun — the solar feminine name of brilliant warm radiance.
What is the spiritual meaning of Shamsa in Ilm ul Huroof?
Sheen radiates, Meem holds in love, Sin expands, Ta manifests. The sun: radiant, loving, expanding, manifest.
