About the name Yousuf

What does the name Yousuf mean?
Yousuf means: Joseph — the South Asian spelling widely used in Pakistan and India. It is a spelling variant of Yusuf — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name يوسف.
Is Yousuf the same as Yusuf?
Yes — Yousuf and Yusuf are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Yousuf a good Islamic name?
Yes, Yousuf is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Yusuf (يوسف) — Joseph — the South Asian spelling widely used in Pakistan and India.
Which spelling is correct — Yousuf or Yusuf?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name يوسف is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
