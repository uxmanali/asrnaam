About the name

Avesta is a Persian name, used across Persian-speaking Muslim communities from Iran through Central Asia and the South Asian Muslim world. The name carries the meaning: The sacred book of Zoroaster; used across Kurdish and Persian communities. Wisdom kept in writing.

Every letter of Avesta in the Arabic script has been read in the classical tradition of Ilm ul Huroof as an ontological principle: the letters do not merely spell the sound of the name, they carry the qualities the tradition ascribes to it. See the letter reading above.

In Islam, the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "You will be called on the Day of Resurrection by your names and the names of your fathers, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud). A well-chosen name is a lifelong invocation of the meaning it carries.
