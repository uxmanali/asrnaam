Dictionary Meaning

What Anmol means

انمول Anmol کا مطلب: بیش قیمت، انمول، جس کی قیمت نہ لگ سکے۔

Anmol is an Urdu-Hindi word meaning "priceless, beyond price, whose value cannot be counted." It is used as both a first name and an adjective in daily Urdu speech; when given as a name, it carries the parent's declaration that this child has no equivalent in value. Used for both boys and girls, though slightly more common for girls in Pakistani-Muslim families.

What Anmol reveals
