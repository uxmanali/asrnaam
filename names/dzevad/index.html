Dictionary Meaning

What Dzevad means

جواد Dzevad کا مطلب: سخی، بہت زیادہ عطا کرنے والا۔ بوسنیائی اسم۔

Dzevad (Bosnian Dževad, with the letter Ž pronounced like French "j") is the Bosnian form of Jawad, from the Arabic root ج-و-د (jāda, to be generous). It is a favoured Bosniak male name carrying the classical Islamic virtue of generosity. Muhammad al-Jawad was the ninth Shia Imam.

Root of Dzevad
