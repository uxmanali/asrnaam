Dictionary Meaning

What Ayyub means

The Prophet of patience — Ayyub (AS), the Quranic Job. Afflicted with severe illness for years, losing wealth, family, and health, he never abandoned his Lord. His prayer is the most concise cry in the Quran: 'And Ayyub — when he called to his Lord: Indeed adversity has touched me, and You are the Most Merciful of the merciful (wa anta arhamu al-rahimin)' (21:83). Allah responded immediately and restored everything.

Ayyub names the man of extraordinary patience — who calls upon Allah in his darkest hour with a prayer that contains no demand, only a statement of his condition and a reminder of divine mercy. Top Pakistani, Arab, and Turkish (Eyup) boys name.
