Dictionary Meaning

What Momina means

مومنہ Momina کا مطلب: ایمان والی، اللہ اور اس کے رسول ﷺ پر یقین رکھنے والی۔

Momina, feminine of mu'min (believer), names the woman of faith. The Quran uses the term repeatedly to describe the community of believing women: 'The believing men and believing women are protectors of one another' (Tawbah 9:71). To name a daughter Momina is to state her identity from the start: she is one of the believing women.
