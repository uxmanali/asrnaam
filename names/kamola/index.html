Dictionary Meaning

What Kamola means

Perfect, complete, the one of total wholeness — from Arabic/Uzbek كامل (Kamil — complete, perfect) in its Uzbek feminine form Kamola. The name carries the quality of the woman of perfect completeness — whose character lacks nothing essential.

Kamola is one of the most popular Muslim girl names in Uzbekistan and used across Central Asia. It combines the Arabic concept of perfection (kamal — completeness, perfection) with the Uzbek feminine naming tradition, creating a name that works beautifully in both Arabic and Uzbek-speaking communities.

The Letters

Reading Kamola through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

كKafFirst · Outward SelfاAlifInteriorمMeemInteriorوWawInteriorلLamInteriorاAlifLast · Resolution

Kamola: Kaf (ك) — divine sufficiency. Alif (ا) — origin. Meem (م) — love and depth. Waw (و) — connection. Lam (ل) — divine authority. Alif (ا) — return. Sufficiency — origin — love — connection — divine authority — return. The complete: drawing from divine sufficiency, originary, held in love, connecting, under divine authority, returning. Double Alif: complete at both ends.

"A name is not a label assigned after the fact. It is a structure of principles that begins its work the moment it is given."

What Kamola asks of its bearer

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Kamola

What does the name Kamola mean?
Kamola means: Perfect, complete — the Uzbek Muslim name of total wholeness. From Arabic kamil (complete, perfect) in Uzbek feminine form. Top Uzbek Muslim girl name. 'This day I have perfected your religion' — Quran 5:3. The perfectly complete woman.
Is Kamola mentioned in the Quran?
Root ك-م-ل (perfection, completeness) is Quranic: 'This day I have perfected (akmaltu) for you your religion' (5:3) — the most celebrated Quranic proclamation of completion. Kamola carries this aspiration to divine completeness.
What does Kamola mean in Urdu?
Kamola in Urdu is کامولا (Kamola) meaning مکمل، کامل — perfect, complete, whole.
Is Kamola a good Islamic name?
Yes, Kamola is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Perfect, complete — the Uzbek Muslim name of total wholeness. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Kamola a girl or boy name?
Kamola is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Perfect, complete — the Uzbek Muslim name of total wholeness.
What is the spiritual meaning of Kamola in Ilm ul Huroof?
Kaf (ك) gives sufficiency, double Alif (ا) gives origin and return, Meem (م) holds in love, Waw (و) connects, Lam (ل) gives divine authority. Complete: sufficient, originary, loving, connecting, authoritative, returning.
