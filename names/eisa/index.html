Dictionary Meaning

What Eisa means

Jesus — from Arabic عيسى (Eisa — the Quranic name of Jesus). Eisa (AS) is one of the five Ulul Azm (Messengers of Firm Resolve). He was born of a virgin birth to Maryam (AS), he spoke in the cradle, he raised the dead, he healed the blind and the lepers — all by Allah's permission. He was raised to Allah and did not die. He will return before the Day of Judgement.

The Quran mentions Eisa 25 times. He is described as 'a mercy from Allah' (19:21), 'the Messiah' (3:45), and 'a word from Allah' (3:45). 'And the Messiah Eisa son of Maryam was only a messenger of Allah and His word which He directed to Maryam' (4:171).

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Eisa

ع-ي-س — Arabic name-root for the prophet Isa (Jesus); traditionally connected by Arab grammarians to whiteness mixed with reddishness or to the Hebrew Yeshua, given as a divine sign.

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Eisa reveals

Ain perceives the unseen sign of the virgin birth, Ya reaches between heaven and earth, Seen seals the secret of the prophet's miraculous coming, and the final Ya yearns for his return at the end of time.

How This Shows Up in Life

Personality & relational pattern

People named Eisa tend to be perceptive and watchful in how they meet people. The Ya that runs through the name points to a reaching, aspirational interior — someone who draws people upward toward what they're moving toward. The closing Ya suggests a person who finishes by reaching toward what comes next.

An interpretive reading drawn from the letters of the name — for reflection, not prediction.

The Letters

Reading Eisa through Ilm ul Huroof

Every letter in Eisa carries a role. Ain opens it outward; the letters between hold the interior; the last letter sets how things land.

عAinFirst · Outward SelfperceivingيYaInteriorreachingسSinInteriorexpansionىYaLast · Resolutionreaching

Ain (ع) — the perceiving spring. Ya (ي) — reaching. Sin (س) — flowing expansion. Ya at rest (ى) — arrived. Perceiving spring — reaching — expansion — arrived. Eisa: perceiving from the living spring, always reaching toward those who need healing, expanding his mercy, completely arrived.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Eisa

What does the name Eisa mean?
Eisa means: Jesus — the Prophet born of a virgin, who will return before the Day of Judgement. Jesus in Islam — 25 Quranic mentions. 'The Messiah, Eisa son of Maryam, was a messenger of Allah and His word which He directed to Maryam' (4:171). Born of a virgin, raised to Allah, will return before Judgement Day.
Is Eisa mentioned in the Quran?
Yes — 25 Quranic mentions. 'O Maryam, Allah gives you glad tidings of a word from Him whose name is the Messiah, Eisa son of Maryam' (3:45). Surah Al-Imran and Surah Maryam both tell his story.

Is Eisa a good Islamic name?
Yes, Eisa is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Jesus — the Prophet born of a virgin, who will return before the Day of Judgement. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
