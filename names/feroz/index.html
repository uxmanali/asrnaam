Dictionary Meaning

What Feroz means

Turquoise, victorious — from Persian فیروز (firuz — turquoise gemstone; also triumphant). The turquoise — the bright sky-blue gem — was among the most prized in the Islamic world. Feroz combines beauty with triumph. Widely used across Iranian, Afghan, Pakistani and Central Asian Muslim communities.

How This Shows Up in Life

The Letters

Reading Feroz through Ilm ul Huroof

Each Arabic letter in Feroz sits in a position. The first opens onto the world, the middle letters hold the inner work, the last settles how things finish.

فFaFirst · Outward SelfopeningيYaInteriorreachingرRaInteriormercyوWawInteriorconnectionزZaynLast · Resolutionadornment

Feroz opens with Fa (ف) — spiritual opening. Then Ya (ي) — reaching. Then Ra (ر) — mercy in motion. Then Waw (و) — connection. Then Zain (ز) — adornment. The turquoise: opening spiritually, reaching, moving with mercy, connecting, adorning what it touches. Turquoise adorns everything it is set in.
