Dictionary Meaning

What Aws means

Gift, wolf — from root أ-و-س (awa — to give as a gift). أوس (Aws — gift, the giving one; also wolf in some interpretations). The Banu Aws was one of the two major Arab tribes of Madinah (Yathrib) who became the Ansar — the Helpers — who welcomed the Prophet ﷺ and the Muslim emigrants. The Aws were fierce warriors who had the courage to take on anyone, and generous givers who fed and housed those who came to them. Several companions bore this name.

The Letters

Reading Aws through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

أAlifFirst · Outward SelfوWawInteriorسSinLast · Resolution

Alif (a) — origin. Waw (w) — connection. Sin (s) — flowing expansion. Origin — connection — expansion. The gift: from origin, connecting through the gift, expanding in generosity.

About the name Aws

What does the name Aws mean?
Aws means: Gift, wolf — the companion-poet name of the Ansar of Madinah. From root أ-و-س (gift, the giving one). The Banu Aws — one of the two Ansar tribes who welcomed the Prophet ﷺ to Madinah. The Helpers who gave everything.
Is Aws mentioned in the Quran?
The Ansar are celebrated: 'And those who believed and emigrated and fought in the cause of Allah, and those who gave shelter and aided — it is they who are the true believers' (8:74). The Aws are among the Ansar.

Is Aws a good Islamic name?
Yes, Aws is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Gift, wolf — the companion-poet name of the Ansar of Madinah. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Aws a boy or girl name?
Aws is traditionally a Muslim boy name meaning Gift, wolf — the companion-poet name of the Ansar of Madinah.
What is the spiritual meaning of Aws in Ilm ul Huroof?
Alif grounds in origin, Waw connects, Sin expands. The gift: originary, connecting, expanding.
