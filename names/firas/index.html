Dictionary Meaning

What Firas means

Firas (فِراس, firās) is an Arabic masculine name meaning perspicacity, keen insight, the gaze of a lion. The root ف-ر-س is the same root that gives faras (horse) and firāsa — the discernment by which a person reads truth from outward signs. The Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "Beware the firāsa of the believer, for he sees with the light of Allah" (Tirmidhi). The name was famously borne by Firās ibn al-Naḍr, a Companion of the Prophet, and by the Umayyad-era warrior-poet Firās ibn al-Khaẓraj. In Sufi commentary, firāsa is one of the marks of a heart purified by remembrance — the saint perceives through what is hidden. Firas remains a popular masculine name across the Arab world, especially in the Levant and the Maghreb, often chosen by parents who wish their son both moral courage and the inner sight that distinguishes the believer.

Reading Firas through Ilm ul Huroof
