Dictionary Meaning

What Fawad means

Heart — from root ف-أ-د (fa'ada — the heart, the living pulsing heart). فؤاد (Fu'ad/Fawad — the heart, particularly the living, feeling heart). The Quran uses fu'ad specifically for the perceptive, conscious heart: 'Inna al-sam'a wal-basara wal-fu'ada kullu ula'ika kana anhu mas'ula — Indeed the hearing, sight, and heart (fu'ad) — all of those will be questioned' (17:36). And: 'Wa af'idatuhum hawa' — and their hearts were empty' (14:43).

Fawad became internationally famous through Fawad Khan — the Pakistani actor and singer who became the most successful Pakistani crossover to Indian Bollywood, making Fawad a widely recognised name across both communities. The name is one of the top searched Muslim boy names in Pakistan.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Fawad

ف-ء-د — The root f-'-d gives fu'ad, the heart, specifically the warm beating heart that is the seat of feeling and consciousness in Qur'anic anthropology. The Qur'an addresses it directly: 'the fu'ad did not deny what it saw'.

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Fawad reveals

Fa opens, Hamza is the primordial breath of the heart's first beat, and Dal is the door of return. Alif anchors origin, so the name traces an opening of primordial breath that returns to the door of the living heart.

The Letters

Reading Fawad through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

فFaFirst · Outward SelfؤWawInteriorاAlifInteriorدDalLast · Resolution

Fawad: Fa (ف) — spiritual opening. Waw (و) — connection. Alif (ا) — origin. Dal (د) — returning door. Spiritual opening — connection — origin — returning door. The heart: opening spiritually (the heart is the spiritual opening), connecting all that passes through it, rooted in origin, always returning through the door. The heart opens, connects, is originary, and always returns.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Fawad

What does the name Fawad mean?
Fawad means: Heart — the living beating heart, the seat of emotion and consciousness. From root ف-أ-د (the living perceptive heart). 'The heart (fu'ad) did not lie' (53:11). 'The heart will be questioned' (17:36). Fawad Khan — Pakistani actor/singer — made this name globally famous.
Is Fawad mentioned in the Quran?
Yes — fu'ad (the heart) appears in the Quran: 'The heart (fu'ad) did not lie about what it saw' (53:11). 'Indeed the hearing, sight, and heart (fu'ad) will all be questioned' (17:36). Fawad/Fu'ad is a directly Quranic word.
What does Fawad mean in Urdu?
Fawad in Urdu is فواد (Fawad) meaning دل، قلب — heart, the seat of emotion and soul.
Is Fawad a good Islamic name?
Yes, Fawad is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Heart — the living beating heart, the seat of emotion and consciousness. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Fawad a boy or girl name?
Fawad is traditionally a Muslim boy name meaning Heart — the living beating heart, the seat of emotion and consciousness.
What is the spiritual meaning of Fawad in Ilm ul Huroof?
Fa (ف) opens spiritually, Waw (و) connects, Alif (ا) grounds, Dal (د) returns. The heart: spiritually open, connecting, originary, always returning.
