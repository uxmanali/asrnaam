Dictionary Meaning

What Akif means

عاکف Akif کا مطلب: اعتکاف کرنے والا، اللہ کی عبادت میں مشغول۔ رمضان کے آخری عشرے کی سنت۔

Akif, from the Arabic root ع-ك-ف ('-k-f, "to devote oneself, to remain constant"), names the one who commits himself to devotion. Its most specific Islamic use is for the one who performs i'tikaf – the Sunnah practice of retreating into the mosque for the last ten days of Ramadan, devoted exclusively to worship. As a given name, Akif is a favoured choice for a son whose parents hope for a life of constancy in remembrance.

Arabic Root

Root of Akif

ع-ك-ف (akafa, to devote oneself). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Akif reveals

عَاكِف
Ain ع perception, the seeing eye.
Alif ا the vertical axis.
Kaf ک sufficiency, "kun" – Be.
Fa ف opening, the burst of speech.

Ain perceives, Alif grounds, Kaf brings divine sufficiency, Fa opens. A name of perceiving-and-grounding, becoming-and-speaking.
