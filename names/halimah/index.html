Section 1 · Arabic Root

Where the Name Comes From

ح-ل-م

ح-ل-م. Root h-l-m carries forbearance, gentleness, and patient self-mastery. The feminine halimah names one who embodies this clemency.

Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Halimah weaves earth and water, the elements of steadiness and mercy, patience and feeling. The person is asked to be both root and river, a slow, sheltering presence. Running through the reading is Al-Hamid, the Praiseworthy, meaning the bearer is trusted with the trust to receive genuine praise, not flattery, joined by Al-Tawwab, the Relenting, carried as the readiness to return and be returned to. A person named Halimah holds their people through seasons.

Section 4 · What Families Say

What People Usually Mean by This Name

Gentle, patient. Singapore/Malay -ah form of Halima. Halimah Yacob (born 1954) was Singapore's 8th President (2017-2023), the first female President and first Malay President of Singapore. Her prominence made Halimah one of the most recognized Malay Muslim names globally. Same blessed foster mother of the Prophet ﷺ.
