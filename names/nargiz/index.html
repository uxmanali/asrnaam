Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Nargiz

Persian origin — narges, narcissus flower. Through Persian poetry the narcissus becomes the emblem of the longing eye that does not blink. Azerbaijani/Turkic spelling Nargiz.

Dictionary Meaning

What Nargiz means

Narcissus flower — from Persian نرگس (Nargis/Nargiz — the narcissus, the jonquil flower). The narcissus in Persian and Central Asian tradition is celebrated for its pure white petals and intoxicating fragrance — in Persian Sufi poetry, the nargis (narcissus eye) is compared to the eye of the beloved, large and deep and beautiful.

Nargiz is one of the top Muslim girl names in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Azerbaijan, and across Central Asia and the Caucasus. Also used as Nargis in Pakistan and India. The name carries the quality of the pure white flower whose beauty is gentle, fragrant, and quietly radiant.

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Nargiz reveals

Nun of depth opens, Alif of origin, Ra of mercy, Gaf of creation, Ya of reaching, Zayn of subtle radiance. A flower whose gaze reaches the divine.

People often notice a quiet inward life, thinking long before speaking in those who carry this name.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run watchful, inward, present in a quiet way. The Ra that runs through the name points to forward motion, a restlessness in good ways — someone who tends to lead or set the direction in a group. The closing Sin suggests a person who settles into support — resolves into service.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

The Letters

Reading Nargiz through Ilm ul Huroof

Every letter in Nargiz carries a role. Nun opens it outward; the letters between hold the interior; the last letter sets how things land.

Nargiz: Nun (ن) — primordial depth. Ra (ر) — perpetual mercy. Ghain (غ) — hidden interior. Sin (س) — flowing expansion. Depth — perpetual mercy — hidden interior — flowing expansion. The narcissus: from primordial depth, moving with perpetual mercy, from the hidden interior, flowing outward in beauty. The flower: deeply rooted, merciful, inwardly rich, expansively beautiful.

The Portrait

What Nargiz asks of its bearer

People named Nargiz tend to have a quality of pure, inwardly rich beauty — their beauty comes from a hidden interior and flows outward gently. The Nun holds in depth. The Ghain gives hidden interior richness. The Sin gives flowing expansion.

A Notable Bearer

Nargis Dutt (1929-1981) — legendary Indian-Pakistani actress, one of the greatest performers in South Asian cinema. Her name Nargis (narcissus) perfectly described her eyes, celebrated as the most beautiful in Indian film.

Famous Bearers

No widely-attested historical or contemporary figure carries this exact spelling of Nargiz. The name's resonance comes from its meaning and its letters — not from a single famous bearer.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Nargiz

What does the name Nargiz mean?
Nargiz means: Narcissus flower — the pure white flower of Central Asian beauty. From Persian nargis (narcissus flower). Pure white flower of deep fragrance. Top Muslim girl name in Uzbekistan, Tajikistan, Azerbaijan. Also Nargis in Pakistan/India diaspora.
Is Nargiz mentioned in the Quran?
The fragrant plants and flowers are celebrated as divine blessings: 'And fragrant plants (raihan)' (55:12). 'So which of the blessings of your Lord will you deny?' (55:13). The narcissus carries this quality of blessed, pure, fragrant creation.
What does Nargiz mean in Urdu?

Is Nargiz a good Islamic name?
Yes, Nargiz is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Narcissus flower — the pure white flower of Central Asian beauty. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Nargiz a girl or boy name?
Nargiz is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Narcissus flower — the pure white flower of Central Asian beauty.
