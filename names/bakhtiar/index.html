Dictionary Meaning

What Bakhtiar means

بختیار Bakhtiar کا مطلب: قسمت والا، خوش نصیب، جسے قسمت نے ساتھی بنایا۔ فارسی سے آیا۔

Bakhtiar, from the Persian bakht (fortune, fate) + yar (companion, possessor), literally means "fortune-companioned" – the one whose companion is good fortune. The name carries the specific sense not just of being lucky but of being intimately accompanied by luck. In the Chishti Sufi tradition, it is borne by Khwaja Qutbuddin Bakhtiar Kaki, the great master buried at Mehrauli in Delhi.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Bakhtiar reveals

بَخْتيار
Ba ب the vessel, containment.
Kha خ the hidden interior.
Ta-light ت the multiplied surface.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Alif ا the vertical axis.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.

Ba contains, Kha holds hidden interior, Ta manifests, Ya reaches, Alif grounds, Ra moves. A name whose letters trace: contain the hidden fortune, manifest it, reach with it, ground it, and move mercifully through it.
