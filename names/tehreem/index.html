Dictionary Meaning

What Tehreem means

تحریم Tehreem کا مطلب: حرمت والی، مکرم۔ ہر وہ چیز جو مقدس ہو۔

Tehreem, from the Arabic root ح-ر-م (haram, ihram, hurmah), names the sacred and honoured one. The name shares its root with the Haramayn (the two sanctuaries of Mecca and Medina) and with Surah At-Tahrim (Chapter 66) of the Quran. Set apart, honoured, protected.

Root of Tehreem

ح-ر-م (to make sacred). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Tehreem reveals

تَحْرِيم
Ta-light ت the multiplied surface.
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Meem م the maternal, water.

Ta manifests, Ha carries breath, Ra moves in mercy, Ya reaches, Meem is maternal. Sacred and honoured.
