Dictionary Meaning

What Raheel means

رحیل Raheel کا مطلب: مسافر، سفر کرنے والا، مسلسل حرکت میں رہنے والا۔

Raheel, from the Arabic root ر-ح-ل (r-h-l, "to travel, to depart, to journey"), names the traveller – the one whose life is measured in journeys. In classical Arabic poetry, "raheel" is the departure at dawn of the caravan, the moment before sunrise when the traveller must leave what he has loved. As a given name, Raheel carries this poetry: a life that will not stay in one place, that is always in service to what lies ahead.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Raheel reveals

رَحيل
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Lam ل authority, the extended reach.

Ra sets motion (motion is his name), Ha carries breath, Ya reaches, Lam extends into authority. A name whose four letters read as: motion, breath, reaching, extended authority. The traveller's alphabet.
