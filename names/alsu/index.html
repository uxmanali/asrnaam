Dictionary Meaning

What Alsu means

آل صو Alsu کا مطلب: گلابی پانی، حسین۔ تاتاری مسلم اسم۔

Alsu is a Volga Tatar female name meaning "rose-water", from Tatar al (pink, rose-colored) + su (water). It is one of the most beloved Tatar girls' names and has spread across Russian-speaking Muslim communities. In Cyrillic: Алсу. The famous Russian pop singer Alsu (Alsou Ralifovna Safina) carries this name.
