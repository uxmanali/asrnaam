Dictionary Meaning

What Fatuma means

East African Swahili form of Fatima. Dominant in Kenya (6M Muslims), Tanzania (20M Muslims), Uganda, Mozambique, and across the African continent's Swahili-speaking Muslim communities. Fatuma is how the Prophet's daughter's name sounds in Swahili phonology. An enormous distinct audience from all other Fatima/Fatimah/Fathima pages.

The Letters

Reading Fatuma through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

Fa (spiritual opening) — Alif (origin) — Ta (divine goodness) — Meem (love) — Ta (manifestation). Pure: open, originary, good, loving, manifest.

"A name is not a label assigned after the fact. It is a structure of principles that begins its work the moment it is given."
