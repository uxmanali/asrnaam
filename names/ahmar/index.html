Dictionary Meaning

What Ahmar means

احمر Ahmar کا مطلب: سرخ، سرخ رنگ کا، سرخ رو ہونے والا۔ عرب اور پاکستانی خاندانوں میں مستعمل۔

Ahmar, from the Arabic root ح-م-ر (h-m-r, "to be red"), means "red, ruddy, of red complexion." In classical Arabic naming it was used for a child born with rosy cheeks, and later carried a connotation of health, vigour, and the flush of life. In Pakistani-Muslim tradition, Ahmar remains an uncommon but distinguished choice.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Ahmar reveals

أَحْمَر
Alif ا the vertical axis of being.
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Meem م the maternal, water.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.

Alif grounds, Ha encloses breath, Meem holds the maternal, Ra sets motion. A name of grounded, breathing, motion-in-mercy.
