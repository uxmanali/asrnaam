Dictionary Meaning

What Movsar means

موسی Movsar کا مطلب: موسیٰ علیہ السلام کا چیچن اسم۔ جس کو پانی سے کھینچا گیا۔

Movsar is the Chechen form of Musa, the Prophet Moses. The name is one of the most-given male names in Chechen-Ingush families, honoring the Prophet who spoke to Allah, parted the Red Sea, and received the Torah. In Cyrillic: Мовсар.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Movsar reveals

موسى

Waw و the connecting conjunction.
Seen س the flowing surface.

Meem is maternal water, Waw connects, Seen flows, Ya reaches. The water-alphabet of Musa.
