Dictionary Meaning

What Lubaba means

Essence, the innermost, the pure core — from root ل-ب-ب (lubab — the essence, the innermost part, the pure core). لبابة (Lubaba — the one of the innermost essence, the pure core). The lubab is the purest, innermost part of something — the kernel, the heart, the essence stripped of all exterior.

Lubaba al-Kubra (RA) — Lubaba bint al-Harith al-Hilaliyya — was the wife of Abbas ibn Abd al-Muttalib (the Prophet's uncle) and the mother of the great companion Abdullah ibn Abbas (RA), the interpreter of the Quran. She was one of the first women to accept Islam, accepting it alongside Khadija (RA). She was called Lubaba al-Kubra (the Great Lubaba) to distinguish her from Lubaba al-Sughra.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Lubaba

ل-ب-ب — From l-b-b (the kernel, the innermost essence). Lubāba: 'the inner essence of a thing'. Same root as labīb and ūlū al-albāb.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run drawing others in through warmth and attachment. The Ba that runs through the name points to a steady, grounded inner posture that values structure — someone who builds durable relationships from a foundation of service. The closing Ta suggests a person who completes things cleanly, with few loose ends.

The Letters

Reading Lubaba through Ilm ul Huroof

Read letter by letter, Lubaba unfolds in stages: the first marks how the bearer appears; the middle ones hold what is private; the last says how the journey closes.

لLamFirst · Outward SelfauthorityبBaInteriorvesselاAlifInteriororiginبBaInteriorvesselةTaLast · Resolutionmanifestation

Lubaba: Lam (ل) — divine authority. Ba (ب) — vessel. Alif (ا) — origin. Ba (ب) — vessel again. Ha (ه) — divine presence. Divine authority — vessel — origin — vessel — divine presence. The essential: under divine authority, a vessel for the purest essence, rooted in origin, a vessel again, breathing divine presence. Double Ba: she holds the essence doubly — she is twice a vessel for what is most pure.

The Portrait

What Lubaba asks of its bearer

People named Lubaba tend to hold what is most essential and pure — their presence gets to the core of what matters. The Lam gives divine authority. The double Ba makes them a double vessel: they hold the essence twice. The Ha breathes divine presence.

A Notable Bearer

Lubaba bint al-Harith al-Hilaliyya (RA) — one of the first women to accept Islam, wife of Abbas (the Prophet's uncle), mother of Ibn Abbas (RA) — the greatest interpreter of the Quran among the companions.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Lubaba

What does the name Lubaba mean?
Lubaba means: Essence, the innermost — the companion of the Prophet and mother of Ibn Abbas. From root ل-ب-ب (essence, innermost core). Lubaba bint al-Harith (RA) — one of the first women to accept Islam, mother of Ibn Abbas (RA). 'Signs for people of pure understanding (uli al-albab)' — Quran 3:190.
Is Lubaba mentioned in the Quran?
Root ل-ب-ب (essence, innermost) gives ulu al-albab — people of pure understanding — mentioned throughout the Quran: 'Indeed in that are signs for people of pure understanding (uli al-albab)' (3:190). Lubaba carries the aspiration to this Quranic quality of pure, essential understanding.
What does Lubaba mean in Urdu?
Lubaba in Urdu is لبابہ (Lubaba) meaning جوہر، اندرونی حقیقت — essence, the innermost core.
Is Lubaba a good Islamic name?
Yes, Lubaba is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Essence, the innermost — the companion of the Prophet and mother of Ibn Abbas. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Lubaba a girl or boy name?
Lubaba is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Essence, the innermost — the companion of the Prophet and mother of Ibn Abbas.
What is the spiritual meaning of Lubaba in Ilm ul Huroof?
Lam (ل) gives divine authority, double Ba (ب) holds the essence twice, Alif (ا) grounds, Ha (ه) breathes presence. The essential: authoritative, doubly holding, originary, divinely present.
