About the name Miriam

What does the name Miriam mean?
Miriam means: Mary — the Hebrew form widely used by Muslim communities. It is a spelling variant of Maryam — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name مريم.
Is Miriam the same as Maryam?
Yes — Miriam and Maryam are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Miriam a good Islamic name?
Yes, Miriam is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Maryam (مريم) — Mary — the Hebrew form widely used by Muslim communities.
Which spelling is correct — Miriam or Maryam?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name مريم is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
