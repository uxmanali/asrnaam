Dictionary Meaning

What Tooba means

Goodness, blessedness, the tree of Paradise — from the root ط-ي-ب (tayyib — to be good, to be pure, to be wholesome). The Quran uses the exact word tuba in Surah al-Ra'd: 'Those who believe and do righteous deeds — tuba (good news, blessedness, the tree of Paradise) is for them and a beautiful place of return' (13:29).

Classical commentators give multiple readings of tuba in this verse. Ibn Abbas read it as 'good news for them'; al-Tabari recorded the reading 'the goodness of life'; and several Hadith collections (Ahmad, Bukhari, Muslim) describe Tuba as a tree in Paradise so vast that a rider would not cross its shade in a hundred years, and from which the cloth of Paradise is woven. The Prophet ﷺ said: 'Tuba is a tree in the Garden the size of a hundred years — the clothes of the people of the Garden are made from its bark' (Ahmad).

The name Tooba (or Tuba) as a girls name carries that Quranic blessing — the woman called by name into the goodness Allah promised His believers. Top Pakistani-Urdu, Turkish, and Arab girls name. Variants: Tooba, Tuba, Touba, Toba. The Arabic is طُوبَى.
