Dictionary Meaning

What Perihan means

Queen of the fairies — Persian/Turkish compound of parī ('fairy') and khan ('lord, queen').

Pronunciationpe-RI-han  ·  pe·ri·han  ·  3 syllables

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Perihan reveals

Ba (Pa) of foundation opens, Ra of mercy, Ya of reaching, Ha of presence, Alif of origin, Nun of depth. A fairy-soul made present.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run reserved but warm once approached. The Ra that runs through the name points to forward motion, a restlessness in good ways — someone who tends to lead or set the direction in a group. The closing Nun suggests a person who ends inwardly, contemplatively.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Perihan
