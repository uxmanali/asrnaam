Dictionary Meaning

What Juwayriyah means

Little woman — diminutive of jaria (woman). جويرية (Juwayriyah — little woman). Juwayriyah bint al-Harith (RA) — Mother of Believers. She was taken captive after the Battle of Banu al-Mustaliq. She came to the Prophet ﷺ seeking help with her ransom. He offered to pay her ransom and marry her. When the companions heard that she was now a Mother of Believers — the Prophet's wife — they said: 'Her people cannot be slaves to the Prophet's in-laws' — and freed all 100 captives they held. The Prophet said: 'I know of no woman who was a greater blessing to her people than Juwayriyah.'.

The Letters

Reading Juwayriyah through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

جJeemFirst · Outward SelfوWawInteriorيYaInteriorرRaInteriorيYaInteriorة?Last · Resolution

Jeem (j) — comprehensive beauty. Waw (w) — connection. Ya (y) — reaching. Ra (r) — mercy. Ya (y) — reaching again. Ta (t) — manifestation. Beauty — connection — reaching — mercy — reaching again — manifestation. The blessing: comprehensively beautiful, connecting, doubly reaching, merciful, manifest.

"A name is not a label assigned after the fact. It is a structure of principles that begins its work the moment it is given."

About the name Juwayriyah

What does the name Juwayriyah mean?
Juwayriyah means: Little woman — Mother of Believers, whose marriage freed a hundred captives. Mother of Believers. 'I know of no woman who was a greater blessing to her people than Juwayriyah' — the Prophet ﷺ. Her marriage led to the freeing of 100 Muslim captives.
Is Juwayriyah mentioned in the Quran?
'He it is who strengthened you with His help and with the believers' (8:62). Juwayriyah's marriage resulted in the freedom of 100 Muslims — a single woman becoming a cause of liberation for a hundred people.

Is Juwayriyah a good Islamic name?
Yes, Juwayriyah is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Little woman — Mother of Believers, whose marriage freed a hundred captives. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).

Juwayriyah is traditionally a Muslim girl name meaning Little woman — Mother of Believers, whose marriage freed a hundred captives.
What is the spiritual meaning of Juwayriyah in Ilm ul Huroof?
Jeem gathers beauty, Waw connects, double Ya reaches, Ra moves with mercy, Ta manifests. The blessing: beautiful, connecting, reaching, merciful, manifest.
