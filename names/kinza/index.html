Dictionary Meaning

What Kinza means

کنزہ Kinza کا مطلب: خزانہ، قیمتی نگہداشت شدہ چیز۔

Kinza names the treasure, the precious thing kept safe. Al-Kanz al-Makhfi ('the hidden treasure') is a famous Sufi phrase drawn from a Hadith Qudsi in which Allah says: 'I was a hidden treasure, and I loved to be known, so I created the creation to be known.' To name a daughter Kinza places her in that resonance.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Kinza reveals

کِنْزَة
Kaf ک sufficiency, "kun" – Be.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.
Zay ز the fine ornament.
Ha-light ه presence, breath.

Kaf brings divine sufficiency, Nun holds hidden treasure, Zay ornaments, Ha carries presence. The alphabet of the hidden treasure.
