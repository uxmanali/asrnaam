Dictionary Meaning

What Sohaib means

صہیب Sohaib کا مطلب: سرخی مائل بھورا۔ حضرت صہیب رومی رضی اللہ عنہ، جلیل القدر صحابی۔

Sohaib was the personal name of Suhayb al-Rumi (Suhayb the Roman), a famous Companion of the Prophet. Born in Iraq, captured as a child by Byzantine soldiers, he made his way to Mecca where he embraced Islam early and endured torture. Remembered for giving up all his wealth to be allowed to make the Hijra. The Prophet said: 'The trade of Suhayb has profited.'

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Sohaib reveals

صُهَيْب
Sad ص sincerity, unbending upright.
Ha-breath ه presence, breath.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Ba ب the vessel, containment.

Sad begins in unbending sincerity, Ha carries breath, Ya reaches, Ba contains. The profitable trade the Prophet praised.
