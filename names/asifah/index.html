Dictionary Meaning

What Asifah means

آصفہ Asifah کا مطلب: مضبوط، پُر ارادہ خاتون، انتظام کرنے والی۔

Asifah, the feminine of Asif (the name of Sulaiman's wise vizier Asif ibn Barkhiya in Islamic tradition), carries the sense of one who is strong-willed, resolute, and capable of taking responsibility. In Pakistani-Muslim naming, Asifah is a favoured girls' name for a daughter whose parents hope she will grow into that self-possession and quiet capability.

Arabic Root

Root of Asifah

ا-ص-ف (asafa, to be strong-willed). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Asifah reveals

آصِفَة
Alif ا the vertical axis of being.
Sad ص sincerity, the unbending upright.
Fa ف opening, the burst of speech.
Ha-light ه presence, the light "he".

Alif grounds in origin, Sad rises in unbending sincerity, Fa opens into speech, Ha closes in presence. A name of origin, upright integrity, and quiet radiance.
