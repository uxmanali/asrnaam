Dictionary Meaning

What Nuha means

Intellect, reason, deep understanding — from root ن-ه-ي (naha — to be of deep understanding, to have keen intellect). نهى (Nuha — intellect, the people of deep understanding). The Quran uses this word: 'Inna fi dhalika la-ayat li-uli al-nuha — Indeed in that are signs for the people of intellect (uli al-nuha)' (20:54 and 20:128).

Nuha appears directly in the Quran as one of the most celebrated qualities — the faculty of deep understanding that allows one to see the signs of Allah in creation. The 'uli al-nuha' (people of nuha) are honoured in the Quran as those who can perceive what others cannot. Nuha names the woman who possesses this Quranic quality of deep, penetrating understanding.

The Letters

Reading Nuha through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

نNunFirst · Outward SelfهHaInteriorىYaLast · Resolution

Nuha: Nun (ن) — primordial depth. Ha (ه) — divine presence. Ya at rest (ى) — arrived. Primordial depth — divine presence — arrived. Deep understanding: coming from primordial depth, breathing divine presence, and arriving complete. True intellect comes from the deepest source, is divinely animated, and arrives at its conclusion fully.

The Portrait

What Nuha asks of its bearer

People named Nuha tend to have a quality of penetrating, deep understanding — they see what others miss. The Nun holds in primordial depth: their understanding comes from somewhere very deep. The Ha breathes divine presence: their intellect is divinely animated. The Ya at rest arrives complete: their understanding arrives fully.
