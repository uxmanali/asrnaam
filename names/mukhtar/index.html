Dictionary Meaning

What Mukhtar means

The chosen one — from the root خ-ي-ر (khayara — to choose, to prefer the better). The passive participle mukhtar means 'the one who has been chosen', reserved for the very best. The Quran uses the same root in 'And your Lord creates what He wills and chooses (yakhtaru)' (28:68) — the verse that grounds the entire concept of divine election.

One of the classical titles of the Prophet ﷺ is al-Mukhtar — the Chosen One — alongside al-Mustafa (the Selected) and al-Habib (the Beloved). The combined title Muhammad Mukhtar appears in classical poetry (madih nabawi) celebrating the Prophet's place as Allah's choice from the entire chain of prophets.

In Islamic history the name was carried by al-Mukhtar al-Thaqafi (d. 67 AH), who led an uprising in Kufa to avenge the martyrdom of al-Husayn at Karbala — a controversial figure in Islamic history. The standalone Mukhtar is widely used and carries the simple meaning of the chosen, preferred boy. Top Arab, Maghrebi, Pakistani, and West African boys name (especially in Senegal, Mali, and Mauritania, where it is among the most common male names). Variants: Mukhtar, Mokhtar, Muhtar, Muchtar (Indonesian). The Arabic is مُخْتَار.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Mukhtar

خ-ي-ر — from kh-y-r, to choose. Mukhtār is the chosen, the selected, the elect.

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Mukhtar reveals

Meem of love opens, Kha of hidden depth, Ta of completion, Alif of origin, Ra of mercy. A chosen one held in hidden depth.

People often notice a quiet inward steadiness, the family centre even at a distance in those who carry this name.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run soft and loving, instinctively familial in their bearing. The Kha that runs through the name points to a sense of the hidden and the unspoken; intuitive — someone who carries other people's confidences well. The closing Ra suggests a person who lets endings push into the next beginning.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

Read letter by letter, Mukhtar unfolds in stages: the first marks how the bearer appears; the middle ones hold what is private; the last says how the journey closes.

The Portrait

What Mukhtar asks of its bearer

The reading of Mukhtar begins with Meem: an outward self made of love. Beneath the surface, Kha brings an interior of secrets and subtlety; Ta brings an interior of becoming. These are what the name does without ever announcing it. Mukhtar ends in Ra — the life resolves through motion. The work of the name is to let the chosen one live through every letter — so that his life takes the same shape as his name.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Mukhtar

What does the name Mukhtar mean?
Mukhtar means: Chosen, selected — the one chosen for a great role. From root kh-t-r (chosen). Al-Mukhtar al-Thaqafi — who avenged Hussain (RA). Widely used Pakistan, India, Turkey, Arab world.
Is Mukhtar mentioned in the Quran?
'And your Lord creates what He wills and chooses' (28:68). Mukhtar carries this quality of being divinely chosen for a role.
What does Mukhtar mean in Urdu?
Mukhtar in Urdu is مختار (Mukhtar) meaning منتخب، چنیدہ — chosen, selected.
Is Mukhtar a good Islamic name?
Yes, Mukhtar is an excellent Islamic name. It carries the meaning: Chosen, selected — the one chosen for a great role. The Prophet ﷺ said: "You will be called by your names on the Day of Resurrection, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud).
Is Mukhtar a boy or girl name?
Mukhtar is traditionally a Muslim boy name meaning Chosen, selected — the one chosen for a great role.
What is the spiritual meaning of Mukhtar in Ilm ul Huroof?
Meem holds in love, Kha gives hidden depth, Ta gives divine goodness, Alif grounds, Ra moves with mercy. Chosen: loving, deep, good, originary, merciful.
