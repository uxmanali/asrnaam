Dictionary Meaning

What Jabir means

One who heals, who sets bones — Jabir ibn Abdullah (RA), one of the most prolific hadith narrators with 1,540 hadith. He participated in 19 battles with the Prophet. The famous hadith of the feast at the trench (Battle of Khandaq): Jabir had only a small goat and a bag of barley — the Prophet fed the entire army of 1,000 from this tiny provision. He also narrated the last public sermon the Prophet gave.
