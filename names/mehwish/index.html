Dictionary Meaning

What Mehwish means

Moonlike beauty — from Persian/Urdu مہوش (mehwish — as beautiful as the moon, moonlike). Mah (moon) + wush (suffix meaning like, similar to). The woman whose beauty is compared to the full moon — the highest standard of feminine beauty in Persian and Urdu poetic tradition.

Mehwish is one of the top 10 most searched Muslim girl names in Pakistan, and among the most searched Pakistani Muslim girl names globally through the South Asian diaspora. Mehwish Hayat — the celebrated Pakistani actress and global Goodwill Ambassador — has brought the name extraordinary visibility.

PronunciationMEH-wish  ·  meh·wish  ·  2 syllables

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Mehwish reveals

Meem of love opens, Ha of presence, Waw of connection, Sheen of radiance. A moonlike radiance carried through connection.

People often notice an inward gravitational pull that draws people back for ease in those who carry this name.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run soft and loving, instinctively familial in their bearing. The Ha that runs through the name points to an open, breath-like quality alive in conversation and feeling — someone who energises the people around them. The closing Sheen suggests a person who finishes with a sense of light and recognition.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

The Letters

Reading Mehwish through Ilm ul Huroof

Mehwish reads as a sequence. The opening letter (Meem) sets how the name meets the world; the middle shapes interior life; the closing letter governs how matters resolve.

Mehwish opens with Meem (م) — love and depth. Then Ha (ه) — divine presence. Then Waw (و) — connection. Then Sheen (ش) — the solar, radiant witness. Love and depth — divine presence — connection — solar radiance. Moonlike beauty: held in love and depth, breathing divine presence, connecting all it illuminates, and radiating like the sun. The moon connects everything it lights — and its light comes from the solar source.
