Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Hafsah

What does the name Hafsah mean?
Hafsah means: The guardian — the Arabic-faithful spelling of the Prophet's wife's name. It is a spelling variant of Hafsa — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name حفصة.
Is Hafsah the same as Hafsa?
Yes — Hafsah and Hafsa are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Hafsah a good Islamic name?
Yes, Hafsah is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Hafsa (حفصة) — The guardian — the Arabic-faithful spelling of the Prophet's wife's name.
Which spelling is correct — Hafsah or Hafsa?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name حفصة is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
