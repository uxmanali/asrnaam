Dictionary Meaning

What Ramadan means

رمضان Ramadan کا مطلب: رمضان المبارک، اسلامی سال کا نواں مہینہ، فرض روزوں کا مہینہ۔ اکثر رمضان میں پیدا ہونے والے لڑکوں کا نام رکھا جاتا ہے۔

Ramadan, from the Arabic root ر-م-ض (r-m-d, "to be scorched by heat"), is the ninth month of the Islamic lunar calendar and the holiest month in Islam: the month of the Quran's revelation, of fasting from dawn to sunset, of Laylat al-Qadr. As a given name, Ramadan is common across the Arab world (especially in Egypt) for boys born during the month. In Pakistan and India the name is less common as a first name but retains all of the month's weight and reverence.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Ramadan reveals

رَمَضَان
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Meem م the maternal, water.
Dad ض the taut resistance, uniquely Arabic.
Alif ا the vertical axis.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.

Ra sets motion-in-mercy, Meem is maternal source, Dad brings taut resistance (fasting itself), Alif grounds in origin, Nun closes into preserved treasure. A name whose letters trace fasting: motion, source, taut restraint, origin, hidden reward.
