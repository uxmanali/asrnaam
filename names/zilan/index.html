About the name

Zilan is a Kurdish name, kept in use by Kurdish Muslim families across Iraq, Iran, Turkey, and Syria. The name carries the meaning: Kurdish; a name of a river valley in eastern Anatolia, also a martyr.

Every letter of Zilan in the Arabic script has been read in the classical tradition of Ilm ul Huroof as an ontological principle: the letters do not merely spell the sound of the name, they carry the qualities the tradition ascribes to it. See the letter reading above.

In Islam, the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ said: "You will be called on the Day of Resurrection by your names and the names of your fathers, so give yourselves good names" (Abu Dawud). A well-chosen name is a lifelong invocation of the meaning it carries.
