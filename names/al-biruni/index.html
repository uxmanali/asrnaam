Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Al-Biruni is grounded in earth, the element of stability, patience, and quiet holding. The letters ask their bearer to be reliable rather than showy, to keep the household of the heart in order. Stitched through this reading is Al-Ahad, the One, meaning the person carries a singular, undivided presence. A person named Al-Biruni builds ground under other people's lives.

Section 5 · Bearers

Who Has Carried This Name

  • Abū al-Rayḥān Muḥammad ibn Aḥmad al-Bīrūnī (973 – c. 1048 CE · Khwarezm / Ghazni / India). Al-Bīrūnī accompanied Maḥmūd of Ghazna's expeditions into India and there composed Kitāb fī Taḥqīq mā li-l-Hind, a careful study of Indian sciences, religions, and customs based on direct contact with Sanskrit scholars. His al-Qānūn al-Masʿūdī is an astronomical encyclopedia. He calculated the Earth's radius using a method involving sighting from a mountain summit. He wrote on pharmacology, mineralogy, geography, and the rotation of the Earth, recognising the latter as a theoretical possibility, though not asserting it.

Frequently Asked Questions

About Al-Biruni

Who was Al-Biruni?
Al-Biruni (973 – c. 1048 CE) was The Polymath of Khwarezm. Abū al-Rayḥān Muḥammad ibn Aḥmad al-Bīrūnī. Active in Khwarezm / Ghazni / India, field: Astronomy, mathematics, geography, history, anthropology.
What does the name Al-Biruni mean?
Al-Biruni means: Nisba from bīrūn. Persian for 'outer suburb', referring to the outer district of Kath, his birthplace in Khwarezm.
Is Al-Biruni an Islamic name?
Yes. Al-Biruni is a recognised Arabic name of significant historical weight in the Muslim world, borne by The Polymath of Khwarezm.
What does Al-Biruni mean in Urdu?
Al-Biruni in Urdu is written البیرونی. نسبت 'بیرون' سے، جو فارسی میں بیرونی محلے کے معنی میں ہے, یعنی اس کی پیدائشی جگہ کاتھ کا بیرونی علاقہ۔
What region was Al-Biruni associated with?
Khwarezm / Ghazni / India.
