Dictionary Meaning

What Memoona means

Blessed, fortunate, the safely-arrived — the Pakistani Urdu spelling of Maymunah (ميمونة), from the Arabic root ي-م-ن (yamana — to be on the right, to be fortunate, blessed). The right side in Arabic and in the Quran is the side of blessing: the People of the Right (ashab al-yamin) are the dwellers of Paradise: 'And the companions of the right — what are the companions of the right?' (56:27).

The most famous bearer is Maymunah bint al-Harith (RA), the last of the Mothers of the Believers — the eleventh and final wife of the Prophet ﷺ. The Prophet married her in the month of Dhul-Qi'dah after the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah, and she lived until 51 AH (around 671 CE), passing away at the same place where she had been married — Sarif, near Makkah. Aisha (RA) said of her: 'By Allah, Maymunah was the most God-fearing among us and the most kind to her relatives.' The Prophet's nephew Ibn Abbas (RA) was Maymunah's nephew through her sister, and he learned much of his Hadith from her household.

The double-o is the South Asian Urdu transliteration; Arabic prefers Maymunah. Top Pakistani Muslim girls name. Variants: Maymunah, Maimoona, Memoona, Maimuna.

The Letters

Reading Memoona through Ilm ul Huroof

Every Arabic name is a sequence of ontological principles. The first letter governs the outward self. The middle letters govern the interior. The final letter governs how things resolve.

مMeemFirst · Outward SelfloveیYaInteriorreachingمMeemInteriorloveوWawInteriorconnectionنNunInteriordepthہHaLast · Resolutionpresence

Meem (love) — Ya (reaching) — Meem (love again) — Waw (connection) — Nun (depth) — Ha (divine presence). Blessed: doubly loved, reaching, connecting, deep, divine.

"A name is not a label assigned after the fact. It is a structure of principles that begins its work the moment it is given."

The Portrait

What Memoona asks of its bearer

At the opening of Memoona stands Meem: an outward self made of love. At the interior, Alif brings an originary axis at the interior; Meem brings an interior of love and water. These are the inward currents the name carries. Memoona ends in Alif — everything resolves back to the source. Read together, the letters ask her to embody blessed, fortunate as a lived structure, not merely a definition.
