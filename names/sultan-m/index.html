The letters of Sultana unpack as follows in the classical Science of Letters:

سلطانة

Seen (س): the tooth, precision, the fine edge  ·  Lam (ل): extension, reaching outward  ·  Ta (ط): depth, the ground-strike, the plow  ·  Alif (ا): origin, standing upright, the first breath  ·  Nun (ن): the fish, knowledge, the inkwell  ·  Ta marbuta (ة): the closing, the feminine seal
