Dictionary Meaning

What Tahmina means

تہمینہ Tahmina کا مطلب: شاہنامہ فردوسی کی رومانوی کردار، رستم کی محبوبہ اور سہراب کی والدہ۔

Tahmina is a Persian name traced to Ferdowsi's Shahnameh (The Book of Kings). In that epic, Tahmina is the princess of Samangan, beloved of the great hero Rustam, and mother of Sohrab. The name carries the weight of that legend: a woman of noble bearing, of tragic love, of a mother whose son becomes his father's equal and unknown adversary. In Pakistani and Iranian Muslim families, Tahmina is a name given with deep cultural resonance.

Arabic Root

Root of Tahmina

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Tahmina reveals

تہمینہ
Ta-heavy ط manifestation, the appearance-becoming-real.
Ha-breath ه presence, the light "he".
Meem م the maternal, water.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.

Ta manifests, Ha carries presence, Meem is maternal, Ya reaches, Nun closes into hidden treasure. A name of manifest presence, maternal aspiration, and preserved depth.
