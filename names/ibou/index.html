Dictionary Meaning

What Ibou means

Ibrahim/Yusuf — Senegalese affectionate short form. Ibou is the Senegalese contracted form for either Ibrahim (most common) or Yusuf. Ibou Sawaneh — Gambian footballer. Used across Senegal and Gambia. The short, warm form of the great Prophet names — carried with family affection.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Ibou

إ-ب-ر — Senegalese Wolof familiar form of Ibrahim (and sometimes Yusuf), used as a stand-alone name across Senegal and the Gambia. Same root as Ibrahim — the Hebrew ʾ-b-r-h-m, 'father of many'.

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Ibou reveals

The Alif (ا) is the origin standing upright, the Ba (ب) the foundation that grounds, and the Waw (و) the connection that joins. The letters together suggest a self whose origin and ground are joined in connection.

How This Shows Up in Life

Personality & relational pattern

People named Ibou tend to be open and self-possessed in how they meet the world. The Ba that runs through the name points to a steady, grounded inner posture that values structure — someone who builds durable relationships from a foundation of service. The closing Waw suggests a person who ties threads together at the close.

An interpretive reading drawn from the letters of the name — for reflection, not prediction.
