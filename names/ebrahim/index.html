About the name Ebrahim

What does the name Ebrahim mean?
Ebrahim means: Father of nations — the Persian and South African spelling. It is a spelling variant of Ibrahim — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name إبراهيم.
Is Ebrahim the same as Ibrahim?
Yes — Ebrahim and Ibrahim are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Ebrahim a good Islamic name?
Yes, Ebrahim is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Ibrahim (إبراهيم) — Father of nations — the Persian and South African spelling.
Which spelling is correct — Ebrahim or Ibrahim?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name إبراهيم is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
