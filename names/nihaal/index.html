Dictionary Meaning

What Nihaal means

نہال Nihaal کا مطلب: شاداب، فارسی میں نوجوان پودا۔

Nihaal, from the Persian nihāl (young plant, sapling, fresh shoot), names the child as green new growth. The word carries the flavour of springtime, of new life pushing up through the earth. Favoured Indo-Persian name for both boys and girls, especially in modern Pakistani-Muslim naming.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Nihaal reveals

نِهَال
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.
Ha-breath ه presence, breath.
Alif ا the vertical axis.
Lam ل authority, the extended reach.

Nun holds hidden treasure, Ha carries breath, Alif grounds, Lam extends. Preserved potential, the sapling that reaches upward.
