Ilm ul Huroof reading

The letters of Zahra unpack as follows in the classical Science of Letters:

زهراء

Zayn (ز): adornment, the light that decorates  ·  Ha (ه): the soft breath, the sigh  ·  Ra (ر): the head, the direction taken  ·  Alif (ا): origin, standing upright, the first breath  ·  Hamza (ء): the glottal spark, the sudden interruption
