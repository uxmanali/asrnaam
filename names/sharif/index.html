Dictionary Meaning

What Sharif means

شریف Sharif کا مطلب: اعلیٰ نسب کا، معزز، شرافت والا۔ آل رسول ﷺ کی نسل کے لیے استعمال ہوتا ہے۔

Sharif, from the Arabic root ش-ر-ف (sh-r-f, "to be high, exalted, noble"), names the person of noble lineage and honourable conduct. In Islamic tradition, "Sharif" is the honorific for descendants of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ through Hasan (as opposed to Sayyid, through Husayn). More broadly, Sharif is one of the most respected male names across the Arab and South Asian Muslim world.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Sharif reveals

شَرِيف
Sheen ش radiance, the spreading light.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Fa ف opening, the burst of speech.

Sheen radiates, Ra sets motion, Ya aspires, Fa opens: a name that begins in radiance and ends in opening. Nobility that moves outward, always aspires higher, and speaks.
