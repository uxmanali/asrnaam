Dictionary Meaning

What Maisam means

Mark, brand of beauty — from root w-s-m (wasama — to mark with beauty). Maisam al-Tammar — an early companion martyr from Kufa who was crucified for his loyalty. Iranian and Lebanese girls name. The quality: the mark of beauty that Allah places on a person — visible to those who know how to see.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run soft and loving, instinctively familial in their bearing. The opening Meem carries the pattern inward — a deep capacity for love and a strong tie to family and inner faith, and someone who tends to become the emotional centre of the people around them. The repeated Meem at both ends suggests a person who completes things in love — few endings are bitter.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

What Maisam asks of its bearer

What carries Maisam into the world is Meem: an outward self made of love. Through the interior, Ya brings an interior of strong selfhood; Seen brings an interior of asking.
