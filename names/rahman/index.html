Dictionary Meaning

What Rahman means

رحمٰن Rahman کا مطلب: اللہ تعالیٰ کا صفاتی نام "الرحمٰن"، سب پر رحم کرنے والا۔ عام طور پر "عبدالرحمٰن" کے ساتھ استعمال ہوتا ہے۔

Rahman, from the Arabic root ر-ح-م (r-h-m, "to have mercy, to be gentle"), is one of the two names of Allah invoked in every "Bismillah" and thousands of times daily in Muslim prayer: Al-Rahman, "The Most Merciful." The intensive form (fa'laan) denotes an all-encompassing mercy that reaches every creature, believer or not. As a human name, the classical rule is to use it with the "Abd" prefix: Abdul Rahman (servant of the Most Merciful), which the Prophet said is one of the two names most beloved to Allah.

Arabic Root

Root of Rahman

ر-ح-م (rahima, to have mercy). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Rahman reveals

رَحْمٰن
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Meem م the maternal, water.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.

Ra is motion-in-mercy itself, Ha carries breath, Meem is maternal source, Nun closes into hidden treasure. The name of divine mercy in its four essential letters.
