Wafik Meaning in Urdu (وفیق)

وفیق. Harmonious.

The name Wafik (وفيق) is written in Urdu as وفیق. In the South Asian Muslim tradition, Wafik carries the meaning of Harmonious. In the South Asian Muslim tradition, Wafik is given with intention, the letters and the meaning together shape the bearer.

Section 1 · Arabic Root

Where the Name Comes From

و-ف-ق

و-ف-ق, from w-f-q, to grant success. Wafīq is the one whose path is granted success. Same root as tawfīq.

Section 3 · Synthesis

What This Reading Says About You

Wafik's letters draw from many elements at once, a full palette, not a single note. This is a name that asks its bearer to hold contradictions gracefully: warmth and steadiness, movement and depth.
