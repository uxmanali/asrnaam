Meaning

What Zehra means

Zehra means: Radiant — the Turkish and Bosnian spelling. The name comes from the Arabic زهراء and carries the same deep Islamic meaning regardless of which spelling is used.

Zehra is the Turkish and Balkan spelling of Zahra — one of the most common girls' names in Turkey, Bosnia, and Albania. The 'e' reflects the Turkish vowel harmony system applied to the Arabic sound. Zehra/Zahra: the Radiant — the title of Fatima (RA), daughter of the Prophet ﷺ, whose face shone with an inner light that others could see.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Zehra

ز-ه-ر — Turkish/Persian form of Zahrāʾ, from z-h-r, to bloom. Al-Zahrāʾ is the title of Sayyida Fāṭima (RA).

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Zehra reveals

Zayn of subtle radiance opens, Ha of presence, Ra of mercy, Alif. Bloom made present.

People often notice an instinct for adornment, making what they touch more beautiful in those who carry this name.

The Reading

Personality & relational pattern

Bearers of this name often run bright and noticing in how they show up. The Ha that runs through the name points to an open, breath-like quality alive in conversation and feeling — someone who energises the people around them. The closing Hamza suggests a person who lands clearly and definitively.

Read this as a mirror, not a verdict.

Frequently Asked Questions

About the name Zehra

What does the name Zehra mean?
Zehra means: Radiant — the Turkish and Bosnian spelling. It is a spelling variant of Zahra — different English transliteration of the same Arabic name زهراء.
Is Zehra the same as Zahra?
Yes — Zehra and Zahra are different English spellings of the same Arabic name. They carry identical meaning and identical Islamic heritage.
Is Zehra a good Islamic name?
Yes, Zehra is an excellent Islamic name. It is a valid transliteration of Zahra (زهراء) — Radiant — the Turkish and Bosnian spelling.
Which spelling is correct — Zehra or Zahra?
Both are correct. Arabic names can be spelled multiple ways in English because English does not have a standardised system for transliterating Arabic letters. The Arabic name زهراء is the same regardless of English spelling. Use whichever spelling your community and family prefers.
