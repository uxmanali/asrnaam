Dictionary Meaning

What Sebaa means

Sheba — Surah Saba in the Quran (34). The Queen of Sheba accepted Islam at Solomon's invitation. Sebaa names the girl of noble conversion quality. Top Arab girls name.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Sebaa

س-ب-ع — from s-b-ʿ, seven. Sabʿa is the number seven, sacred number in Arabic and Quranic numerology (seven heavens, seven earths).

Ilm ul Haroof Meaning

What Sebaa reveals

Sin of secret opens, Ba of foundation, Alif of origin, Ain of perception, Alif. The sacred number seven made name.

How This Shows Up in Life

Personality & relational pattern

People named Sebaa tend to be quietly supportive in how they enter a room. The Ba that runs through the name points to a steady, grounded inner posture that values structure — someone who builds durable relationships from a foundation of service. The closing Alif suggests a person who lands things on a clear, singular note.

What Sebaa asks of its bearer
