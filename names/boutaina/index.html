The letters of Boutaina unpack as follows in the classical Science of Letters:

بثينة

Ba (ب): the house, containment, the vessel of meaning  ·  Tha (ث): three points of witness, the abundance  ·  Ya (ي): yearning, the vocative, the reaching-toward  ·  Nun (ن): the fish, knowledge, the inkwell  ·  Ta marbuta (ة): the closing, the feminine seal
