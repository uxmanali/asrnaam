Dictionary Meaning

What Ilnur means

النور Ilnur کا مطلب: وطن کی روشنی، دیس کا نور۔ ولگا تاتار مسلم اسم۔

Ilnur is a Volga Tatar / Bashkir male name meaning "light of the homeland", from Tatar il (country, homeland) + Arabic nūr (light). It is one of the most-given male names in Russia's Volga-Ural Muslim republics (Tatarstan, Bashkortostan) since the late 20th century. In Cyrillic: Ильнур.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Ilnur reveals

إِلْنُور
Alif إ origin.
Lam ل authority, the extended reach.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.
Waw و the connecting conjunction.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.

Alif grounds, Lam extends, Nun preserves, Waw connects, Ra moves in mercy. The alphabet of land-light.
