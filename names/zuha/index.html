Dictionary Meaning

What Zuha means

ضحی Zuha کا مطلب: چاشت کا وقت، صبح کی روشنی۔ قرآن مجید کی سورۃ الضحیٰ کا نام۔

Zuha, from the Arabic root ض-ح-ي (d-h-y, "morning brightness"), names the forenoon light – the moment when the sun has fully risen and the day is at its clearest. Allah swears by this hour in Surah Ad-Duha (Chapter 93): "By the morning brightness, and by the night when it is still, your Lord has not abandoned you, nor is He displeased." As a girls' name, Zuha carries the entire promise of that sura: a name given to a daughter who is the parent's own answer to a long night.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Zuha reveals

ضُحى
Dad ض the taut resistance, uniquely Arabic.
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.

Dad brings taut resistance (the light forcing itself past night), Ha carries breath, Ya reaches. Three letters that read as: light against dark, breath of dawn, reaching into day.
