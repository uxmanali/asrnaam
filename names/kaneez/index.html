Dictionary Meaning

What Kaneez means

کنیز Kaneez کا مطلب: خادمہ، خدمت گزار۔ عام طور پر "کنیز فاطمہ" کے ساتھ استعمال ہوتا ہے یعنی حضرت فاطمہ کی خدمت گزار۔

Kaneez, from the Persian kaniz (maid, servant), is a girls' name that carries a specific devotional weight. It is almost never used alone; the classical form is "Kaneez Fatima" (servant of Fatima, i.e., of the Prophet's daughter), "Kaneez Zahra," or "Kaneez Sakina." A parent naming their daughter Kaneez is dedicating her, symbolically, to the service of the Prophet's household. Common especially in Shia families.

Arabic Root

Root of Kaneez

From the Persian kaniz: maid, servant, handmaid. This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

What Kaneez reveals
