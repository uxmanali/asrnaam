Dictionary Meaning

What Zohaib means

ذوہیب Zohaib کا مطلب: چھوٹا سونا، قیمتی۔

Zohaib, the diminutive form of dhahab (gold) in Arabic, means "little gold" or "precious." The diminutive is affectionate: what a parent might call a beloved child, dear as gold. Popular in Pakistan.

Arabic Root

Root of Zohaib

ذ-ه-ب (dhahaba, to go, gold). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Zohaib reveals

ذُهَيْب
Dhal ذ the reference, the pointing.
Ha-breath ه presence, breath.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Ba ب the vessel, containment.

Dhal points to what is remembered, Ha carries presence, Ya reaches, Ba contains. Precious thing preserved.
