Dictionary Meaning

What Khwaja means

خواجہ Khwaja کا مطلب: استاد، شیخ، مرشد۔ خصوصاً چشتی صوفی سلسلے میں اہم اصطلاح۔

Khwaja is a Persian honorific title meaning "master, lord, teacher, honoured elder." In the Chishti Sufi tradition, "Khwaja" is the title borne by the great masters: Khwaja Moinuddin Chishti of Ajmer, Khwaja Nizamuddin Auliya of Delhi, Khwaja Qutbuddin Bakhtiar Kaki of Delhi. When given as a personal name, Khwaja carries the weight of that Sufi lineage: a parent placing their son under the aegis of the great masters.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Khwaja reveals

خواجہ
Kha خ the hidden interior.
Waw و the connecting conjunction.
Alif ا the vertical axis of being.
Jeem ج gathering, the meeting-place.
Ha-light ه presence, the light "he".

Kha begins in the hidden interior, Waw connects, Alif grounds, Jeem gathers, Ha carries presence. A name that starts inward, extends outward, gathers others, and becomes presence.

Famous Bearers

People who have carried the name Khwaja

  • Khwaja Nizamuddin Auliya. 1238–1325. Great Sufi master of Delhi.
