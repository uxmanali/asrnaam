Dictionary Meaning

What Medina means

مدینہ Medina کا مطلب: مدینہ منورہ، نبی کریم ﷺ کا شہر۔ بوسنیائی اور چیچن مسلم اسم۔

Medina names the daughter after al-Madinah al-Munawwarah ("the Illuminated City"), the city of the Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. After Mecca it is the second-holiest city in Islam, where the Prophet built the first mosque and is buried in his Rawdah. Both Bosniak and Chechen Muslim families give the name Medina to daughters as a form of du'a, may she carry the light of the Prophet's city with her.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Medina reveals

مَدِينَة
Meem م the maternal, water.
Dal د the door, the transition.
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Nun ن the inkwell, hidden treasure.
Ha-light ة presence, the multiplied surface.

Meem is maternal source, Dal opens the door, Ya reaches, Nun preserves, Ha carries presence. A name of maternal source opening the door of the Prophet's city.
