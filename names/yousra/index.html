Dictionary Meaning

What Yousra means

یسری Yousra کا مطلب: آسانی، اللہ کی طرف سے آسانی۔ قرآن مجید کی اصطلاح۔

Yousra (Arabic yusrā) is the Quranic word for divinely-granted ease. The Prophet said: 'Islam is ease.' The Quran says: 'Allah desires ease for you, and does not desire hardship' (Baqarah 2:185). Naming a daughter Yousra is a du'a for her: may her whole life be eased.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Yousra reveals

يُسْرَى
Ya ی aspiration, reaching.
Seen س the flowing surface.
Ra ر motion, mercy in movement.
Alif ا the vertical axis.

Ya reaches, Seen flows, Ra moves in mercy, Alif grounds. Ease as a posture.
