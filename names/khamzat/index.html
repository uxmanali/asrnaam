Dictionary Meaning

What Khamzat means

حمزت Khamzat کا مطلب: شیر، طاقتور۔ حضرت حمزہ رضی اللہ عنہ کا چیچن اسم۔

Khamzat is the Chechen-Ingush form of Hamza, honoring the Prophet's uncle Hamza ibn Abdul-Muttalib, the "Lion of Allah." It is one of the most iconic Chechen male names, worn by rulers, resistance leaders, and fighters through several centuries of North Caucasus Muslim history. In Cyrillic-writing Chechen tradition it is spelled Хамзат.

Arabic Root

Root of Khamzat

ح-م-ز (hamiza, to be strong). This root is the linguistic-spiritual family of the name; other names sharing the same root carry a related weight.

Ilm ul Huroof Meaning

What Khamzat reveals

حَمْزَت
Ha-breath ح the breath of enclosure.
Meem م the maternal, water.
Zay ز the fine ornament.
Ta-light ت the multiplied surface.

Ha carries breath, Meem is maternal, Zay ornaments, Ta manifests. A name of breath, mother-source, ornament, and manifestation. The Chechen lion.
