Dictionary Meaning

What Fatima means

The shining, the weaned one — Fatima is the dominant global English spelling, used by Arab communities worldwide, South Asian Muslims, and the global Muslim diaspora. Fatimah (with h) captures classical/Gulf searches; Fatima captures the rest.

'Fatimah is part of me — whoever harms her harms me' — the Prophet (Bukhari). The same Al-Zahra, the same leader of the women of paradise — in the spelling that reaches the majority of global Muslim searches. Top 1 or 2 Muslim girls name globally in every Muslim community.

Arabic Root Meaning

Root of Fatima

ف-ط-م — From f-ṭ-m, to wean — Fāṭima is 'the one who is weaned (from the fire of Hell)' per a hadith tradition.
